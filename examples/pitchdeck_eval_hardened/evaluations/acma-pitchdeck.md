# Acma

**Verdict:** GO

## Summary

Acma is a compliance automation platform specifically designed for AI-first companies, addressing the growing complexity of regulatory requirements in AI development. By integrating directly into development workflows like GitHub and GitLab, Acma transforms complex policies into actionable, machine-readable guardrails that help engineering teams ship compliant AI products efficiently. 

The startup targets mid-market AI SaaS companies in the EU and US with a serviceable market of approximately 18,000 companies, projecting an average contract value of 30,000 EUR annually. Their solution addresses critical pain points for CTOs, legal teams, and enterprise customers by automating compliance checks, reducing audit times, and providing evidence-based documentation.

With an impressive founding team from Stripe, GitLab, and DeepMind, Acma has already secured 12 design partners, converted 6 to paying customers, and achieved 37,000 EUR in monthly recurring revenue. Their roadmap includes expanding EU AI Act coverage, launching a risk scoring engine, and building a partner program with auditors and security firms.
