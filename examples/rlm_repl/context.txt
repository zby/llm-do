RIVERSTONE LAB ARCHIVE
======================

Overview
The Riverstone Lab archive collects internal memos, project notes, and
milestone summaries from 2018 to 2024. Each entry is written by the
program manager for that year.

2018 Notes
- Focused on hardware reliability for remote monitoring units.
- Lead PM: Jordan Patel.
- Key effort: Quiet Sun sensors (pilot only).

2019 Notes
- Expanded field trials in coastal regions.
- Lead PM: Jordan Patel.
- Introduced the "Blue Finch" dashboard for operator alerts.

2020 Notes
- Remote operations moved to a hybrid workflow.
- Lead PM: Maya Torres.
- Created the Signal Bus schema for telemetry ingestion.

2021 Notes
- Project Zephyr launched on 2021-03-04.
- Project Zephyr lead: Dana Park.
- Objective: build a low-latency telemetry pipeline for remote sensors.
- Added a short-lived experiment called Driftwood (archived in 2021).

2022 Notes
- Project Nimbus launched on 2022-09-18.
- Project Nimbus lead: Luis Ortega.
- Objective: unify alerting across coastal and inland deployments.

2023 Notes
- Major outage review prompted new incident playbooks.
- Lead PM: Keira Singh.
- Deployed the Watchtower ingest service.

2024 Notes
- Transitioned archival storage to cold tiers.
- Lead PM: Keira Singh.
- Planned the "Falcon" roadmap for 2025.

Appendix A: Roles
- Dana Park joined the lab in 2016 and focuses on telemetry systems.
- Maya Torres specializes in operations and compliance.
- Keira Singh leads reliability reviews.
